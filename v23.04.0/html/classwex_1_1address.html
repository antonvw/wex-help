Parameters

addressthe address, being a string containing:
  • a normal line number
  • a defined marker, like 'x, or '<: begin of selection and '>: end of selection
  • $ : last line
  • . : current line
  • or a combination of these, using + or -
  • or empty, call set_line afterwards
